About Venbaah

Venbaah is a prestigious international journal dedicated to Tamil language and literary studies. We publish high-quality research articles, critical essays, and scholarly works that contribute to the understanding and preservation of Tamil literature and culture.

Our mission is to provide a platform for scholars, researchers, and literature enthusiasts to share their insights and contribute to the rich tapestry of Tamil literary scholarship.
